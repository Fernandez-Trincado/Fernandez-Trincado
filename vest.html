EOSQS - the Equatorial Wide-field Optical Survey for QSOs variables and short-period variable Stars

Overview

EOSQS is a multi-epoch photometric astronomical survey of short-period variable stars in the Milky Way (b < 16 deg.) using the 1.0/1.5-m Jurgen Stock Telescope/Camera Schmidt, 1.0-m Reflector Telescope of the National Observatory of Venezuela (NOV), 50-cm Reflector Telescope of XXXX Argentina. The EOSQS collaboration consists of researchers from over 10 institutions around the world and is coordinated by J. G. Fernandez-Trincado. xxxxx
