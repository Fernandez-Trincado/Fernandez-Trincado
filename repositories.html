Reduction Images of the Reflector 1m, configuration f/5, National Astronomical Observatory of Venezuela

CIDA-Reflector1m_f5: is the Pipeline beta version developed and maintained by José G. Fernández Trincado for the Reduction, Aperture Photometry and Astrometric Calibration of the images .fits of the Reflector 1m of the National Astronomical Observatory of Venezuela.
  • Basic Reduction: Bias and Synthetic Flat. PyRAF
    • Run1..5
  • Aperture Photometry: PyRAF
    • Run_6-Aperture_Photometry.py
  • Astrometric Calibration: WCStools
    • Astrometry_V2.py
Course: Procesing Astronomical Images from Python
